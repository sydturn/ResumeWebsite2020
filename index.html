 Introduction


Hey there!

My name is Sydney. I am a software developer who loves to learn and teach. One of my favorite things that I have had the pleasure of experiencing in my life was running the Computer Science Club at Algoma University for a year. I was able to meet new people, plan all kinds of tech events, and learn a lot! I think that it is super important to start teaching kids how to program when they are young. Programming is so rewarding, and is such a huge part of our tech dominated world. A lot of people (my past-self included) think that learning to program is something completely unattainable, like rocket science; they don't realize that with a little bit of help and practice they too can learn to create amazing things!

Something interesting about myself is that I initially started off studying law (as you can see from my education). The study of law is super interesting, but I remember in my 4th year of Law I walked into the Computer Science Lab at my University to meet a friend and saw a woman presenting a simple website that she had developed for a class. I was completely amazed that she was able to create something so unique and I thought to myself that it was like a painting that you could interact with. I had told her how amazing she was for making something like that and she responded "oh it's really not that hard" and began to talk a little bit about what developing her website had entailed. I asked the friend I was meeting there to show me how to program and he taught me how to make a simple tip calculator using Python. It was amazing! From then on I was hooked, and the rest is history.

If you are interested in learning more about my credentials then read on! If you are curious about the world of programming and fancy a chat, reach out to me using any of the contact methods listed there on the left!
